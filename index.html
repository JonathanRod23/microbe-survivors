Organism guide

Bacterium
Single-celled prokaryote. Common, steady pressure; antibiotic targets are often bacterial structures.
Virion
Virus particle outside a host cell. Fast and fragile; phages are viruses that infect bacteria.
Fungal spore
Reproductive fungal form. Slow and sturdy; fungi are eukaryotes, so they differ from bacteria.
